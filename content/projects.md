## These Lucky Stars

[theseluckystars.com](https://theseluckystars.com)
[r/theseluckystars](https://reddit.com/r/theseluckystars/)

These Lucky Stars (TLS) is an up and coming tabletop RPG focused on balancing narrative detail with play speed.
The system itself can be adapted to any setting, with modules in the works for sci-fi, medieval fantasy, post-apocalypse, and more.

TLS uses a unique health system that builds the narrative into combat; Injuries matter, teamwork is encouraged, and danger is real and felt. Character creation is left open to the players, no classes to follow or being forced to streamline a build. With 2d6 exploding dice and a player managed Luck mechanic to change the stakes, character and player choices share the weight of every roll.
Buuld a character, run a session. Let us know how it goes.
